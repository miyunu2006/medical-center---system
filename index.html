Meet Our Founder & Chief Practitioner

Dr. Amarasiri Wickramarachchi

Established in 2009, Wound Care & Medical Center was founded with a singular vision: to provide patients with dedicated, highly specialized medical care. Dr. Amarasiri Wickramarachchi is our sole practicing physician, ensuring that every patient receives personalized attention and years of medical expertise.

Whether you require a routine general medical consultation, complex wound care management, or minor surgical procedures, you are in safe, experienced hands. Our dispensary is fully equipped to handle diverse patient needs.

Our Specialized Medical Services

Specialized Wound Care

Advanced treatments for chronic wounds, diabetic foot ulcers, post-surgical wounds, and severe injuries. We provide specialized dressings and sterile cleaning to ensure rapid, painless healing.

Dispensary & Minor Surgeries

We safely perform minor surgical procedures under local anesthesia. This includes cyst removals, precise suturing of lacerations, abscess drainage, and ingrown toenail surgeries in a fully sterilized environment.

General Medicine

Comprehensive consultations for everyday illnesses, viral fevers, chronic disease management (like Diabetes and Hypertension), and overall wellness checks by Dr. Wickramarachchi.
